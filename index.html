because I build gorgeous data visualization projects

I personally harvested this precious data for days; I sent Google Surveys to all my fellow seniors asking who they had been in classes with this year, and followed up with each one of them. I then poured the block of data onto this beautiful graph. If you click a name, you can see what I'm talking about.

because I got into a love triangle with Columbia and NYC*

*I lived in Furnald Hall while attending Columbia's summer program,
and I fell in love with both the campus and the city

because I build gorgeous data visualization projects (part 2)
In 2015 the city of New York released the details of all the 146,087,462 yellow taxi cab trips. In a small tribute to both Columbia and NYC, I set out to visualize the trips that either started or ended around the Morningside Heights campus

because I take Columbia with me wherever I go

I take every chance I get to travel and see new parts of the world. Not only because I like to see new places, but mostly because I value the change in perspective traveling affords me. Every time I step out of an airplane coming from some formerly unknown land, I think and feel ever so slightly different.

That's the kind of college exeprience I want and know Columbia can offer me.

Here I'm standing in front on the imperial palace in Tokyo, Japan on the 15th of April 2017

because I care

I spent all of my free time since I was put on Columbia's waitlist putting together this website from scratch. Why? Because to me Columbia is not just a place to be, it's the place I want to be. This site is my own way to prove I sincerely care about having a Columbia education, and that I'm highly motivated to reach for it.

because I'll be in the class of 2021 before you're done saying the word "admitted"

If I end up with an offer from Columbia, I'm going to accept it in a heartbeat;
because I truly believe it's the place I am most suited for.
